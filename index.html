About Me

I am a Web Editor for Leidos - the prime contractor for the National Science Foundation's United States Antarctic Program (USAP), so I get to support science in Antarctica on a daily basis. I am responsible for managing and maintaining content on our three station intranets as well as the public-facing site USAP.gov.

I have been with the program since June 2016. My first rather large task involved traveling to South Pole Station to roll out the new intranet for station personnel. The timing around this allowed me to spend my 24th birthday at the bottom of the world; I think that's pretty cool.

I have a Bachelor of Science degree in English - Professional Writing with a minor in Human Computer Interaction/User Experience (HCI/UX) from the University of North Alabama (UNA). Prior to recieving my bachelor's degree, I graduated from Calhoun Community College with an Associate of Applied Science degree in Business Administration - Paralegal Studies.
